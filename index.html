About Super Burd PS4

Super Burd PS4 is a collection of one-of-a-kind, hand-drawn artworks built around Super Burd a character I came up with as a kid. Each piece drops Super Burd into a totally different world, moment, or mood. Every drawing is unique. Every scenario tells its own story. Every drawing is one-of-one. No reprints. No copies. Just raw creativity that won’t exist again. If you're a collector who values story, style, and something you can’t just scroll past this is for you. The pieces aren’t cheap, and they’re not supposed to be. They’re priced for what they are: real art, made with time, care, and intent. Don’t sleep on the burd.
